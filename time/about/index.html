“Tick Tock”, where time dances with childhood dreams. Here, memories of laughter and play
intertwine with the passage of time, creating a canvas painted with nostalgia. As kids, time was
like a slinky, twisting and turning with each new adventure. Our toys were more than objects—
they were gateways to magical worlds, fueled by imagination and wonder.

This website is a tribute to those cherished moments, with graphics inspired by the playful
motion of a slinky. Here, time is not just seconds ticking by, but the joy of discovery and the
thrill of exploration. After all, as the poem my mother sang to me reminds us, "twinkle twinkle
little stars, up above where we are," time may pass, but the memories we hold dear will
always shine bright in the constellation of our hearts.
